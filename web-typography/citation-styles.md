MDPI and ACS Style

AMA Style

Chicago/Turabian Style
Roth, Robert E.; Ross, Kevin S.; MacEachren, Alan M.	2015. "User-Centered Design for Interactive Maps: A Case Study in Crime Analysis." ISPRS Int. J. Geo-Inf. 4, no. 1: 262-301.
